I am a PhD student working with Marco Cuturi (Apple MLR & CREST-ENSAE, IP-Paris) on the interplay between optimal transport (OT), generative modeling and representational learning. Broadly speaking, I am eager to demonstrate how OT can be instrumental in addressing a variety of machine learning challenges arising in life-science. Recently, I have been particularly interested in the application of OT in single-cell genomics and from February 2024, I will be visiting Fabian Theis at TUM to explore these ideas further.
